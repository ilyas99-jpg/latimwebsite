FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start my project?

For most projects we can begin within 1–2 weeks of agreement and payment of deposit. Larger civil engineering projects may require a planning period. Contact us for a project-specific timeline.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes — we provide free, no-obligation quotes for all projects. Fill in the contact form above or call us directly. For larger projects a site visit may be required before a detailed quote is produced.

Do you service areas outside Jinja?

Yes. While our base is in Jinja, we serve clients across Uganda including Kampala, Mukono, Iganga, Tororo, and surrounding areas. Travel costs may apply for distant locations — contact us to discuss.

What warranty do you offer on completed work?

All our installations come with a workmanship warranty. Specific warranty periods vary by project type. We also offer ongoing maintenance contracts for CCTV, AC, plumbing, and water systems to keep everything performing at its best.

Can I supply my own materials?

Yes, however we recommend allowing us to source materials as we have established supplier relationships that ensure quality, correct specifications, and often better pricing. We are happy to discuss your preferences during the consultation.

Our Story

Ugandan Builders, World-Class Standards

Latimtech Life & Light Ltd was founded in Jinja, Uganda with a singular vision: to prove that Ugandan engineering firms can deliver world-class results on every project — from the smallest plumbing repair to the grandest luxury installation.

Based at Plot 16, Main Street, Jinja, we have grown from a small technical team into a comprehensive multi-discipline engineering firm, proudly serving clients across Uganda's growing Golden Age construction era.

We believe that "Tasking Tasks with Experience" isn't just a tagline — it's a commitment. Every project we take on receives our full technical expertise, premium materials, and meticulous attention to detail.

Mission & Vision

What Drives Us Forward

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional engineering and construction services that elevate Uganda's built environment — combining world-class technical expertise with locally sourced talent, sustainable practices, and an unwavering commitment to client satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted multi-discipline engineering firm — recognised for quality, innovation, and our contribution to Uganda's Golden Age infrastructure development. Building a better tomorrow, one project at a time.
